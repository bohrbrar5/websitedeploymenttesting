Bohar Singh

I am a Climate Dynamics Ph.D. student at AOES , George mason University, Fairfax. I am working with Prof. Jim Kinter. My current research focuses on the Tropical Intraseasonal variability and its seasonal variation. I am also interested in air-sea intreactions and their role in Tropical Intraseasonal oscillations, role of interhemispheric migration of the mean states of atmospheric circulation, moisture and sea surface temperature in the seasonlity of Tropical Intraseasonal oscillations.
